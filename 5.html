Individual Essay Outline

I have mentioned 3 possible human errors, under the medical ASMIS system:

1. Slips or lapses: Any unintended action or action without planning. On ASMIS system, such human errors can have selected a wrong patient, pressing any wrong button/option, etc.

2. Mistakes: Error of decision making or judgement. For example, wrong judgement to the circumstances of the patient, wrong specialist allocation, etc.

3. Violations: Examples such as non-compliance, employees handling some procedures with shortcuts, circumventions, etc. These violations usually intentional with failures as the outcome.

I also have suggested some solutions.

The feedback was I need to analysis the human errors, including their causes and impacts, but also need to be related to cyber security.

Individual Essay
