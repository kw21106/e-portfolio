Module 1, Unit 3: Peer review and summary

I agree with David's said companies, organisations and or SMEs should collaborate and support together, fight against the cyber security crimes.

And actually it is happening. According to Gerard (2019), two European countries, Denmark and Norway, have started to attract the SMEs of countries to join their cyber security defense programme. Partnership activities including knowledge and experience exchange campaigns, for SMEs to share the experience of being attacked on the internet, how did they recover and minimised the loss, any solution and cyber security measure and policy are deployed after the attack. As Panagiotis has suggested, the Governments also approach the learning programmes and seminars to the CEOs, directors, help them to understand how important to invest on the cyber security to their companies, how can a secured company network environment help them saving money and reserve their reputation; the Governments also help SMEs to budget, plan, provide solution and funding for the SMEs cyber security deployments through the non-profit organisations or companies.

Simon suggested helping companies to defend from the cyber security by using AI, but one of the main difficulties for SMEs to deploy the cyber security measures is the skill shortage. This is not only happening in the SMEs, but even the cybersecurity companies. A report from Scot and May (2020) has stated that 76% of the interviewed companies or their directors feel very difficult to find a cyber security expert working for them, and 72% said they are working on a cyber security project, but cannot hire a suitable expert, at least a year after the project is confirmed. As AI also needs to be designed and deployed by humans, shortage of relevant experts also means more difficult to deploy and maintain a cyber security solution.

In conclusion, not just money, but the knowledge of the senior level and sufficient human resources and talents, are the key to deploy a cyber security solution successfully.
